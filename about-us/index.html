Our Story · 品牌故事

Shanxi Lee's Kitchen — 山西李家厨房 — is a family-run restaurant in Box Hill serving home-style Northern Chinese and Shanxi cuisine. The name reflects our roots: a kitchen built around generous, warming food meant to be shared — lamb spine hot pot, hand-made dumplings, grilled skewers and hearty noodles.

We opened in Box Hill to bring a taste of home to Melbourne's eastern suburbs. We cook the dishes we grew up with — bold broths, fresh ingredients and the flavours of China's northwest — and welcome everyone who walks through our door.

山西李家厨房是一间位于 Box Hill 的家庭式中餐厅，专注山西与西北家常味。店名「李家厨房」代表我们以家宴为核心的理念——羊蝎子火锅、手工水饺、烧烤串与面食，都是适合与朋友、家人一起分享的暖身好菜。我们希望在墨尔本东区，把一份来自北方的家乡味，留给每一位推门进来的客人。
