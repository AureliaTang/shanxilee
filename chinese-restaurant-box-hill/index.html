Shanxi Lee's Kitchen · Box Hill

A Chinese Restaurant in Box Hill for Lamb Hot Pot, Dumplings and Skewers

Box Hill has no shortage of Chinese restaurants, but sometimes you want something a little different from the usual takeaway, noodle shop or spicy hot pot chain. You want a table full of food that feels warm, generous and worth sharing.

Shanxi Lee's Kitchen is a Chinese restaurant in Box Hill serving comforting dishes such as lamb hot pot, dumplings, grilled skewers, noodles and soups. It is a good choice for people who enjoy bold flavours, shared plates and a relaxed local dining experience.

What makes this restaurant different?

The menu is built around hearty dishes rather than delicate small plates. You will find warming hot pot, savoury grilled skewers, comforting dumplings and filling noodle dishes. It is food designed for people who want to eat well and share the meal.

Even if you are not familiar with every dish on the menu, the flavours are easy to enjoy: grilled meat, warm broth, handmade-style dumplings, noodles and savoury sauces.

A good introduction to something new

If you usually order the same Chinese dishes every time, Shanxi Lee's Kitchen is a good place to branch out. Lamb hot pot is a great centrepiece for the table, while skewers and dumplings are easy dishes for everyone to enjoy.

You do not need to know the background of the cuisine to enjoy it. Just come hungry and order a few dishes to share.

What to order first

For a first visit, try:

  • Lamb hot pot as the main dish.
  • Grilled lamb skewers for smoky flavour.
  • Dumplings for comfort.
  • Noodles or soup to round out the meal.

This gives you a good mix of the restaurant's most approachable flavours.

Good for groups and casual dinners

Shanxi Lee's Kitchen is especially well suited to group meals. The dishes are shareable, the flavours are bold and the meal feels relaxed. It is a good option for dinner with friends, family meals or anyone looking for a Chinese restaurant in Box Hill with a strong point of difference.

Visit Shanxi Lee's Kitchen

If you are looking for a Chinese restaurant in Box Hill, visit Shanxi Lee's Kitchen for lamb hot pot, dumplings, skewers and comforting dishes made for sharing.

It is a great place to try something warm, generous and a little different.
