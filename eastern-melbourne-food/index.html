Eastern Melbourne Food Guide: A Share-Friendly Chinese Restaurant in Box Hill

Eastern Melbourne has plenty of food options, but Box Hill remains one of the best places to go when you want a proper Chinese dinner. It is convenient, lively and full of restaurants that are perfect for casual meals and group catch-ups.

Shanxi Lee's Kitchen is a Chinese restaurant in Box Hill serving lamb hot pot, dumplings, grilled skewers, lamb soup, Big Plate Chicken and other comforting dishes made for sharing.

Why Box Hill is a great food destination in the east

Box Hill is easy to reach from suburbs like Doncaster, Blackburn, Burwood, Surrey Hills, Glen Waverley and other parts of Melbourne's east. It is a natural meeting point when your group wants something relaxed but satisfying.

Instead of a quick meal, you can order a table full of dishes and share everything.

What makes Shanxi Lee's Kitchen different?

The food is hearty and social. Lamb hot pot is warm and memorable, dumplings are comforting, skewers bring smoky flavour, and Big Plate Chicken is perfect for a group.

For diners who want something beyond the usual fried rice and sweet-and-sour dishes, this is a good place to try a more interesting Chinese meal without feeling lost.

Best dishes for a first visit

Try lamb hot pot, grilled skewers, dumplings, Big Plate Chicken and a soup or noodle dish. This gives your table a mix of warmth, savoury flavour, texture and comfort.

Good for friends, families and casual dinners

Shanxi Lee's Kitchen suits group dining because the dishes are easy to share. It is a good option for a family meal, birthday dinner, weekend catch-up or relaxed dinner after work.

Visit Shanxi Lee's Kitchen

If you are searching for Eastern Melbourne food and want a Chinese restaurant with warm, generous dishes, visit Shanxi Lee's Kitchen in Box Hill.

It is a great place to gather around lamb hot pot, dumplings and skewers with people you like eating with.
