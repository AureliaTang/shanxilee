North Melbourne Food Guide: A Worth-the-Trip Chinese Dinner in Box Hill

North Melbourne has plenty of cafes, bars and diverse restaurants, but if you want a warm Chinese dinner made for sharing, sometimes it is worth heading to a different suburb.

If you are searching for North Melbourne food but really want a Chinese restaurant suited to group dinners, birthdays and family meals, consider Shanxi Lee's Kitchen in Box Hill.

Why it is worth the trip from the north

Box Hill is one of Melbourne's best-known hubs for Chinese dining. There is hot pot, BBQ, noodles, dumplings and regional flavours — ideal when you want a proper sit-down Chinese meal.

Shanxi Lee's Kitchen serves dishes built for sharing: lamb hot pot, lamb skewers, handmade dumplings, lamb soup and Big Plate Chicken all work well in the middle of the table.

A good weekend food outing

If you usually eat Western food, cafes or quick meals in the north, a weekend trip to Box Hill for Chinese group dining can feel like a welcome change. A pot of hot broth, a few skewers, a plate of dumplings and Big Plate Chicken make for a very satisfying dinner.

What to order first

On a first visit, try:

  • Lamb hot pot
  • Lamb skewers
  • Handmade dumplings
  • Big Plate Chicken
  • Lamb soup
  • Noodles or a cold side dish

This kind of order works well for three to six people sharing.

Good for groups and celebrations

Weekend catch-ups, birthday dinners, family gatherings and small team meals all suit Shanxi Lee's Kitchen. Shared dishes feel more social than everyone ordering separately.

Visit Shanxi Lee's Kitchen in Box Hill

If you are looking for North Melbourne food but want a better fit for Chinese group dining, welcome to Shanxi Lee's Kitchen in Box Hill.

A trip from the north for a lively Chinese table might become your new go-to for group dinners.
