South East Melbourne Food Guide: A Cosy Chinese Dinner Worth the Trip to Box Hill

If you are looking for good food around South East Melbourne, sometimes the best dinner is worth a short drive. Box Hill is one of Melbourne's most popular areas for Chinese food, and it is a great meeting point when everyone is coming from different suburbs.

Shanxi Lee's Kitchen in Box Hill is a good choice when you want a warm, generous Chinese meal made for sharing. The menu includes lamb hot pot, grilled skewers, dumplings, lamb soup, Big Plate Chicken, noodles and other hearty dishes that suit group dining.

Why Box Hill works for a South East Melbourne food trip

If your group is coming from areas like Glen Waverley, Burwood, Clayton, Chadstone, Blackburn or Doncaster, Box Hill is often a convenient place to meet. It has plenty of food options, good public transport access and a lively dining atmosphere.

Instead of choosing a quick takeaway meal, you can sit down, order a few dishes for the table and enjoy a relaxed dinner.

What makes Shanxi Lee's Kitchen a good group option?

The food is built for sharing. Lamb hot pot can sit in the middle of the table, skewers are easy to pass around, dumplings are familiar and comforting, and dishes like Big Plate Chicken make the meal feel generous.

It is a good option for people who want Chinese food that feels warm, savoury and satisfying without needing to know every dish on the menu.

What to order first

A good first-time order could include lamb hot pot, dumplings, lamb skewers, Big Plate Chicken and a noodle or soup dish. This gives your table a mix of warm broth, grilled flavour, comfort food and filling mains.

Best for casual dinners and catch-ups

Shanxi Lee's Kitchen is well suited to weekend dinners, birthday meals, family meals and casual catch-ups with friends. The food is not fussy. It is warm, generous and easy to enjoy together.

Visit Shanxi Lee's Kitchen

If you are searching for South East Melbourne food and want a Chinese dinner that feels cosy and shareable, visit Shanxi Lee's Kitchen in Box Hill.

Bring a few people, order for the table and enjoy a warm Chinese meal built around hot pot, dumplings and skewers.
